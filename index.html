At Mayur Fine Nepali Restaurant located in the heart of Scarborough, we take pride in bringing the true taste of Nepal to your table. Our menu features a wide variety of authentic Nepali dishes such as momo, thakali set, thali, newari khaja, dal bhat, and many more — each prepared with care, hygiene, and freshness in mind. We are committed to serving healthy, flavorful meals that reflect the warmth and richness of Nepali culture. Our friendly staff ensures excellent customer service and a welcoming atmosphere, making every dining experience special. With fast delivery services and over 30,000 satisfied customers giving us a 4.9-star rating on Google Maps, you can trust that your satisfaction is our top priority. Whether you dine in or order online, Mayur Fine Nepali Restaurant guarantees a meal that feels like home.
